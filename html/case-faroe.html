Crafting an Online Experience for Faroe Islands

Web design | Development

Background

The project aims to creating an immersive website that not only informs but also inspires travelers and enthusiasts.

Challenge

Design and develop a captivating website using Bootstrap, HTML, and CSS, complete with a custom logo, engaging infographics, and an animated GIF, to showcase the unique beauty and attractions of the Faroe Islands.

My Role

UX/UI Design, App Development, Content Curator

As a student at OsloMet University, I embarked on a journey to create a captivatin, fictional website that would bring the charm and allure of these islands to the world. Nestled in the North Atlantic, the Faroe Islands are a hidden gem waiting to be explored, and I believe in making their beauty accessible to all.

Design process

I prioritized user-friendliness as a key focus to streamline the process of users accessing the desired information. Throughout the project, I remained mindful of the critical role the first impressions plays, particularly concerning the choice of color palette, imagery, and graphic elements.

The design process began with a thorough research phase, diving deep into Faroese culture, natural wonders, and local stories to inform the website's content and aesthetics.

Moodboard for the project

The website features a color palette centered around black, gray, and vibrant orange. This deliberate choice harmonizes seamlessly with the website's logo, resulting in a visually cohesive and appealing design. The use of black and gray imparts a sense of sophistication and modernity, while the vivid orange adds an element of energy and vibrancy.

Logo development

I have also incorporated a GIF animation, created in Adobe Photoshop, to enhance the website's appeal and dynamism. Challenges

One of the big challenges was finding the right balance between how things looked and how they actually worked. Some design ideas that seemed stunning on paper ended up being tricky to bring to life.

The project was incredibly extensive, unfortunately leaving me with no opportunity to dedicate time to optimizing for faster loading.

Conclusion

In the end, this project wasn't just about making a website. It was a journey of self-discovery and a reminder that when design and development come together, amazing things can happen. It showed me that blending creativity with technical know-how can lead to digital creations that go above and beyond our expectations.
