Development of a company's website

Web Design | Development

Background

Designing and developing a website for a company is a complex endeavor that requires a well-defined process, a deep understanding of the company's goals, audience, brand identity, and choosing appropriate technology stack for website's sustainability.

Challenge

Ensuring that the website functions correctly across different browsers and devices, and the website loads quickly and efficiently, which is vital for user experience.

My Role

UX/UI Design, Front-end Development, Maintenance

As a part-time team member at Dyktig AS, I was entrusted with the task of crafting a company website. The client's vision was clear: they desired a website that exuded professionalism and cleanliness, with a primary focus on spotlighting their valued employees and clients.

Design process

To gather inspiration for the design, I began by examining Dyktig's logo and Facebook profile. I extracted colors, fonts, and also looked at other consulting companies for inspiration. Then, I created simple sketches and discussed them with the client.

Taking our initial ideas to the next level, I brought the project to life by developing a high-fidelity prototype using Figma, allowing the client to visualize the website's potential and functionality.

Tools & Technologies used for creating the website:

  • Figma
  • Vite
  • Styled Components
  • GitHub

View the website

Challenges

Recently, I successfully completed the development phase of the website, employing Vite as the build tool and Styled Components for crafting user interfaces.

Respecting client privacy, while showcasing them on the website is essential. Ensuring that the company has appropriate consent requires careful following of data protection regulations. Plans for future expansion of the website are vital for optimizing the website's architecture. In company's forward-looking strategy, I recognize the significance of planning for the website's future growth and evolution. These plans are instrumental in optimizing the website's architecture, ensuring it remains agile and responsive as the company continues to expand.

Conclusion

I must admit that this is just the beginning of this development journey. I'm thrilled to be part of this ongoing project, where we'll continue to enhance and expand its functionalities, ensuring that it remains a dynamic and effective tool for the company, as well as its stakeholders.
