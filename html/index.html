Who Am I?

Hello! My name is Julia, a UX designer and front-end developer with a bachelors degree in informatics and interaction design. While I was born and raised in Slovakia, my journey led me to Norway, where I've been studying and working for the past decade.

Travelling, one of my passions, got me thinking how services could be changed to make everybody's life better. A great user experience is something that drives me forward, and I thrive by collaborating with diverse cross-disciplinary teams, working with technology, and gaining valuable insights from people.

I have taken various online courses that focus on front-end development, so I could be myself able to translate these insights into real-life services. I think the programming knowledge is also important for a smooth communication with the development team.

  • I like to work structured and to have a plan, but I am also creative problem solver.
  • Working alongside dedicated team members is something I enjoy, although I also value the freedom of independent work and planning.
  • My prior experience in customer experience has allowed me to gain practical knowledge and skills over the years.

I am now eager to take on new challenges and experiences to build upon!
